### **ChoiceMaker Self-Determination Transition Curriculum Modules & Materials**

The  [*ChoiceMaker Self-Determination Transition Curriculum* Matrix](choice-curriculum-matrix.pdf) is a guide for using these lessons. Results from the [*ChoiceMaker Self-Determination Assessment*](ChoiceMakerAssessment2016.pdf) can be mapped onto the Matrix to identify students' self-determination needs.

[Choosing Goals video](https://youtu.be/8BZ2zzK0Df4) is a 10 minute video that may be used to introduce the topic. The video was made by individuals with IEPs. Hair/clothes shows that it is outdated, but the topics are still relevant. Maybe a class or two would want to create an updated video? Many transition skills could be integrated in the endeavor!
