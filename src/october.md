# October

### Self-Advocacy & Self Determination

Young adults will inventory their strengths through the IPLAN strategy. This strategy will help them prepare for their IEPs. They will learn the difference between aggressive, assertive, and non-assertive communication. They will also role-play various situations with their peers.
