**Quick Tips: IEP Meeting 

1. Contact parents for available times and dates 

2. Identify IEP team members 

3. Create a Doodle to get times and dates that work for most 

4. Send out outlook invite/email 

5. Student canva or presentation 

6. Send home NOM 10 days in advance 

7. Finalize former progress reports 

8. Collect input from Gen ed teachers and gather baseline data for new goals 

9. With input from team, write new IEP, PWN if needed 

10. Send home IEP draft within 5 days (do not include PWN) 

11. Get confirmation of who can and cannot attend meeting

12. Appoint meeting notes “taker” 

13. Possibly: Gen ed excusal form 

14. Decide how you are going to show the IEP at the meeting/project or print off 

15. Get a copy of parental rights (school pysch. Will have this) 

16. Student involvement - how is the student involved in their IEP 

17. Create/use IEP Meeting Agenda 

18. Have IEP meeting, PWN, NOM, Excusal signed (all if needed). Double check that someone is taking notes 

19. Attach signed paperwork and possible other needed forms 

20. Finalize IEP and send home final copy to parents**
