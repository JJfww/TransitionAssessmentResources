# April

### Emergency Situations

Several experts from the Lawrence community will be invited to C-Tran to talk about safety during emergency situations. Representatives from the Lawrence Fire Department will come to C-Tran to discuss fire safety and prevention. Finally, the American Red Cross will teach a course about basic injuries and what to do until help arrives. Shelter areas and fire escape routes will be identified throughout these presentations.
