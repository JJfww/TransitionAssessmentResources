# May

### Recreation and Leisure

Throughout the year, young adults in C-Tran will be exploring “3rd Places.” During this month, we will visit many recreation and leisure facilities within the town of Lawrence.  Young adults will vote as a group on 2-3 end of year activities that will take place in the community. Young adults will understand the process of enrollment, fee payment, and activity schedules as they conclude this session.
