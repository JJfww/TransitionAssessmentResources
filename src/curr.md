As you build your schedule, keep in mind the importance of **routines**. Routines and structure serve as a stable foundation for many of our students, offering a sense of predictability and security. This, in turn, fosters a supportive environment in which they can develop critical life skills, adapt to changes, and become more self-sufficient.

Here are some **SAMPLE** schedules to guide you as you begin to build your class's schedule. Of course, you can modifiy and adjust as students begin working in their community work experience, as club activities get added to the calendar, and as you get to know your students and their needs.

#### General example - other school based vocational experiences will be built into the day, but this can be a general schedule/guide for those who are not otherwise engaged.

| Time          | Activity               | Description                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 |
| ------------- | ---------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 8:00 - 8:30   | Breakfast              | Students start their day with breakfast and socialize with their peers. Encourage students who don't eat to walk in the gym or get on an exercise bike in the wellness room.                                                                                                                                                                                                |
| 8:30 - 9:00   | Morning Circle         | Students gather to review the day's schedule, discuss goals, and share any announcements. On Mondays, they will look at the week's schedule and decide on any clubs they wish to participate in and put on their schedule. <br/>Help students build a routine to check their email and use their google or other personal device calendar in addition to a visual schedule. |
| 9:00 - 9:45   | Individual Goals       | Students work on their personal goals with guidance from the instructor or support staff.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   |
| 9:45 - 10:15  | Break (non-tech)       | Students take a 30-minute movement break - they may go outside, walk in the gym, shoot baskets, ride the bicycle, etc. if it's not a scheduled APE day.                                                                                                                                                                                                                     |
| 10:15 - 11:00 | Group Lesson           | The class comes together for a group lesson on topics such as budgeting, meal planning, self-advocacy, workplace basics, etc. Students scheduled to cook can begin prepping and cooking.                                                                                                                                                                                    |
| 11:00 - 11:30 | Lunch                  | Students have a 30-minute lunch break to eat, socialize, and relax.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         |
| 11:30 - 12:00 | Movement Break         | When students finish eating, encourage them to get up and move before afternoon lessons begin.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              |
| 12:00 - 12:45 | Elective               | Students participate in a chosen elective/topic to explore their interests and build additional skills. This could be computer programming, animation,  making movies and learning how to post on social media, reading or listening to audio books, watching you-tube videos on how to make something, etc.j                                                               |
| 12:30 - 1:00  | Individual Practice    | Students engage in individual practice activities based on the skills learned during the group lesson.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      |
| 1:00 - 1:45   | Job Olympics Practice  | The class participates in activities designed to develop vocational  job skills.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            |
| 1:45 - 2:00   | Break (tech)           | Students enjoy a 15-minute break and may use technology for leisure or educational purposes.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                |
| 2:00 - 2:30   | Reflection and Wrap-up | The class gathers to reflect on the day's activities, discuss successes and challenges, and set goals for the next day. Students who had community work shifts will share about their experience for that day.                                                                                                                                                              |
