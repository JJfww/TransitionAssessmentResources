## KU Self-Determination Inventory Assessments

These assessments from the University of Kansas Self-Determination Learning Center focus on choice making, goal setting, and decision making.

- [Self-Determination Inventory: Student Report - English](https://sdis.ku.edu/public-survey/fe36ac84-8342-4c7d-a553-11ec5ebe9073)
  
  Self-report assessment for youth ages 13-22 with and without disabilities.

- [Self-Determination Inventory: Student Report - Spanish](https://sdis.ku.edu/public-survey/244cdf43-0d85-4327-8bc2-679618e016d3)
  
  Spanish version of the student self-report assessment for youth ages 13-22.

- [Self-Determination Inventory: Student Report - American Sign Language](https://sdis.ku.edu/public-survey/f55306cd-55f4-4064-a862-d771b3c7b361)
  
  American Sign Language version of the student self-report assessment, with supports for accessibility.

- [Self-Determination Inventory: Parent Teacher Report](https://sdis.ku.edu/public-survey/a0cb9ea5-5026-4205-84ae-a6d0924fc28d)
  
  Report completed by a parent, family member, or teacher to share their perception of a young person's self-determination.

- [Self-Determination Inventory: Adult Report](https://sdis.ku.edu/public-survey/88dddc60-4127-4497-9183-c2eb65d14118)
  
  Self-report assessment for adults ages 18 and older, with in-text definitions and read-aloud support.

- [KU Self-Determination Assessments Overview](https://selfdetermination.ku.edu/sdlc/assessments/)
  
  Overview page describing the full assessment system and the SDIS data dashboard used by schools, agencies, and researchers.
